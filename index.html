How to play

You get 20 time slots across the semester. Every interaction spends one. Attend classes at Prayukti Bhavan to keep attendance up — drop below 75% and you're debarred before the fest. The Central Library raises CGPA, Milonda's and the Food Tech Building restore energy, and Jheel Paar, OAT, and the Green Zone keep your mood alive.

Money matters: chaa, phuchka, and egg rolls cost cash. Earn it by taking tuition in the Green Zone or collecting your scholarship at Aurobindo Bhavan. Survive the semester with attendance intact and Sanskriti week unlocks — Pronite at OAT, fairy lights at the jheel, and fest points that feed your final verdict.

A fan project made for fun. Not affiliated with Jadavpur University. Building names sourced from the official campus map.
